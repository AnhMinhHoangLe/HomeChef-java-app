Real Recipe:

Ingredients:
1. Egg
2. Water
3. Cooking pot
4. Stove

Procedure:
Step 1: Have a cooking pot, pour cold water into the pot
Step 2: Put the egg into the water
Step 3: Put pot over high heat and bring water to a rolling boil. 
Step 4: Remove pan from heat and cover
Step 5: Let the egg stand in the hot water for 14-17 minutes 
Step 6: Drain egg and put in a bowl filled with ice water 
Step 7: Peel the egg
